# AtliQ Hardware: Consumer Goods Ad-Hoc Insights 📊

## Project Overview
This project focuses on providing data-driven answers to 10 critical ad-hoc business requests for the executive team at AtliQ Hardware[cite: 1]. The goal was to bridge the gap between complex data and actionable business strategy using SQL and interactive visualizations.

## Tech Stack
- **SQL (MySQL):** Complex data extraction, Joins, CTEs, and Window Functions.
- **Power BI:** Data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive dashboard design.
- **Excel & PowerPoint:** For executive-level reporting and presentation.

## Key Business Insights
* **Market Expansion:** Identified key markets in the APAC region where "Atliq Exclusive" operates.
* **Product Growth:** Observed a **36.33% increase** in unique product counts between 2020 and 2021.
* **Manufacturing Costs:** Identified the highest manufacturing cost for "AQ HOME Allin1" and the lowest for "AQ Master mouse".
* **Sales Trends:** Recognized that the **Retailer channel** contributes significantly to total sales, accounting for **73.22%** of gross sales ($1924.17M).
* **Performance Metrics:** Analyzed the March 2020 sales dip and provided context regarding global supply chain impacts.

## Strategic Recommendations
1. **Product Focus:** Prioritize the **Networking** segment as it showed high growth potential.
2. **Cost Control:** Monitor manufacturing costs for **high-end Desktop** models to maintain healthy margins.
3. **Channel Strategy:** Continue optimizing the **Retailer** channel while exploring untapped potential in Direct and Distributor channels.
